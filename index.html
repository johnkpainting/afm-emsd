Welcome to the AFM Electronic Media Resource Center!

We’ve reorganized our contract information to better help employers and musicians alike to find the resources they are looking for.

Information is now organized by recording medium, then further subdivided on sub-pages to help steer you towards the correct agreement, wages, benefits, and filing instructions for your project.

If you’re totally stuck, start with “Where Do I Begin?” below for some background information on why there are so many different agreements, otherwise, start with the broadcast medium that best fits the project you are working on.
